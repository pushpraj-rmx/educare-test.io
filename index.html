How we do it ?

Everyone in this contemporary environment has been introduced to computers since adolescence. Children are familiar with the internet by the time they reach adolescence. They are attracted to laptops and tablets due to the enjoyment factor in gaming, performing other tasks online, and even talking on social media platforms. Through the use of chat, audio, visual, and textual communication occurring simultaneously on an interactive white board, One2One Tutorials harnesses this natural inclination and curiosity of their interest in technology to create a successful engagement between the tutor and the student. In addition to utilizing the many tools on the whiteboard to draw images, upload lectures, and annotate by using different colors and graphs, they may also utilize voice chat, Skype, or Google Meet & Zoom to have discussions. With the use of webcams and two-way communication, the instructor and the students may communicate and study together effectively, forgetting they are in a virtual space and having a live interaction experience.
